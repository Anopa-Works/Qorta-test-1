How QORTA works day-to-day.

A simple, 4-step process designed for speed and clarity.

01

Scan the QR code

Customers scan a unique QR code at their table or at the counter. No apps, no accounts.

02

Browse the menu

A beautiful, high-speed menu opens instantly. Large photos help customers decide faster.

03

Place order

Customers select items and specify their table. The order is sent directly to your staff.

04

Staff serves

Your team receives the order in real-time. They prepare the food and serve it exactly where it's needed.

Why this matters

Fewer mistakes

Digital precision means no more misheard orders or handwritten errors.

Faster decisions

Visual menus help customers choose quickly without needing staff assistance.

Less back-and-forth

Free up your staff to focus on prep and service, not taking down order details.
